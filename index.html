Our Story

Built in Cascais, for those who love Cascais.

Loumi was born from the idea that travelling shouldn't mean putting life on hold. Every residence is designed for those who want to stay longer, feel the city like a local, and come home every day — even when far from their own.

From selecting the spaces to curating every detail, Loumi is building a small collection of residences in Cascais, with the Studio already available and Avilez and Minas on the way.
